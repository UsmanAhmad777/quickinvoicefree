About QuickInvoiceFree

We built the free invoice tool we always wished existed — fast, simple, and genuinely free.

Our Mission

QuickInvoiceFree was built with one goal: to give freelancers and small business owners a professional invoice generator that works instantly, requires no signup, and costs absolutely nothing to use.

We believe that creating a professional invoice should take less than two minutes. You should not need to sign up for expensive software, learn complicated tools, or pay monthly fees just to bill your clients. Invoicing is a basic business function — and basic business functions should be free.

Why We Built This

As freelancers ourselves, we got tired of being forced to create accounts, enter credit card details for "free trials," or use clunky, outdated invoice templates just to send a simple bill. We built QuickInvoiceFree to be everything we wanted — instant, professional, private, and genuinely free.

Your invoice data is processed entirely in your browser. We never see, store, or collect any of the information you enter. No databases, no servers processing your client data — just a fast, clean tool that works for you.

Our tool is used by freelance designers, developers, writers, photographers, consultants, tradespeople, coaches, and small business owners around the world. Whether you send one invoice a month or fifty, QuickInvoiceFree handles it without any friction.
